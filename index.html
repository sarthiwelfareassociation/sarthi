About Us.

A leading organization recognizing the guardianship and care of orphaned children in Poladpur Taluka Activities of the Saarthi Welfare Association.
- Survey of orphaned children
- Give children educational benefits.
- Free Books, Guides and Notebooks, Geometry box, Paper, School backpacks
- Providing school uniforms.
- Free private tuition system for children of 10th and 12th
- Career Guidance and Economic Cooperation
- ST Pass facility to go to school
- Essential dictionary for English
- Bicycle Allocation
- Preparation for examination, questionnaire, sample answer sheets
- Diwali Sweets and Diwali celebrations at home
- Creating Positive Energy by Continuous Dialogue and Guidance
- Mediclaim arrangements
- All children's up to date records
